About FLUID

FLUID is an AI-powered brand and web design agency based in Dubai. We combine brand identity, Webflow development, and AI discoverability into one compounding system we call Fluidity. We work with businesses across the UAE, UK, USA, and beyond.

Most agencies give you a one-time deliverable and disappear. FLUID builds everything as a compounding system through our Fluidity retainer model. We also integrate AI discoverability from day one — so your brand shows up in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini, not just Google.

We're based in Dubai, UAE — but our clients are global. We've worked with brands across the UK, USA, India, and across the GCC. Time zones have never been a problem — we adapt.

We're intentionally selective. We typically run 4–6 active projects at any time to ensure every client gets our full attention. This means we sometimes have a short waitlist — book a discovery call early.

Fluidity

Fluidity is our proprietary monthly retainer model. Each month follows a 4-week cycle: Audit → Strategy → Design → Review. Your brand doesn't stay static — it continuously improves based on data, feedback, and market changes.

Traditional retainers are reactive — you ask for something, they make it. Fluidity is proactive — we're constantly monitoring, strategizing, and improving before you even know you need it. It's the difference between a contractor and a partner.

We require a minimum 3-month commitment (to see meaningful results), after which you can cancel with 30 days notice. We don't lock clients in — if we're not delivering value, you should leave.

Process

Brand Identity: 3–5 weeks. Web Design: 2–4 weeks. Webflow Build: 4–6 weeks. Brand + Web combined: 6–9 weeks. Timelines depend on scope and how quickly feedback is provided. We'll give you a firm timeline during scoping.
