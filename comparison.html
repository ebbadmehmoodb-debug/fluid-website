What you actually spend — and what you actually get.

The full-service agency model.

$25k–$100k

Large teams, long timelines, lots of meetings. Account managers between you and the designers. Multiple handoffs. High overhead baked into the price.

What you don't get: AI discoverability. Compounding improvements. Direct access to senior designers.

FLUID

The compound brand system.

$9.5k–$20k

Senior designers. Direct access. Strategy, design, development, and AI discoverability under one roof. Transparent pricing. Monthly improvements through Fluidity.

What you get extra: A brand that doesn't stop improving after launch.

$2k–$8k

Lower cost, direct contact. Great for small tasks. But limited scope — one skill set, no strategy layer, no AI, no accountability structure, and the risk of disappearing mid-project.
