Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

FLUID ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and contact details when you submit our contact form or book a discovery call
  • Company name and project details you share during onboarding
  • Payment information processed via secure third-party payment processors (we do not store card details)

We also collect information automatically when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages, via analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and deliver our services
  • To send project-related communications
  • To send marketing communications (only with your consent — you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our website and services through analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., Calendly for booking, Stripe for payments, Google Analytics for analytics) who are contractually required to keep your data secure and use it only as directed by us.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Contact form submissions are retained for 24 months. You may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@fluid.agency.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@fluid.agency.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at hello@fluid.agency.

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: May 2026

These Terms and Conditions ("Terms") govern your use of the FLUID website and any services provided by FLUID ("Company," "we," "us"). By accessing our website or engaging our services, you agree to these Terms.

1. Services

FLUID provides brand identity design, web design, Webflow development, web application development, AI discoverability services, and ongoing design retainers ("Services"). Specific deliverables, timelines, and fees are defined in individual project proposals.

2. Payment Terms

Projects require a 50% deposit to begin. Remaining balance is due upon project completion before final files are delivered. Retainers are billed monthly in advance. Late payments may result in work being paused. All prices are in USD unless stated otherwise.

3. Intellectual Property

Upon receipt of full payment, clients receive full ownership of final deliverables. FLUID retains the right to display completed work in our portfolio unless explicitly agreed otherwise in writing. Source files are included only where specified in the project proposal.

4. Revisions

Each project includes two structured revision rounds per deliverable as stated in the proposal. Additional revisions are billed at $150 USD per hour. Revisions must be submitted as consolidated feedback (not rolling individual notes).

5. Client Responsibilities

Clients agree to provide accurate briefs, timely feedback (within 5 business days of each submission), and all necessary content, assets, and access required to complete the project. Delays caused by the client may extend timelines accordingly.

6. Cancellation

Projects cancelled before delivery are subject to a kill fee equivalent to 25% of the total project value plus all work completed to date at our hourly rate. Retainers may be cancelled with 30 days written notice after the minimum commitment period.

7. Limitation of Liability

FLUID's total liability for any claim shall not exceed the total fees paid for the specific service giving rise to the claim. We are not liable for indirect, consequential, or incidental damages of any kind.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how FLUID uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you interact with them.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics). These are anonymized and help us improve the site.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. Only active if you consent.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. You can also opt out of Google Analytics at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

4. Contact

Questions about our cookie use? Email hello@fluid.agency.
