OUR STORY

Started with one question.

Why do great products fail online while mediocre ones dominate? The answer is almost always brand — or rather, the absence of one.

We started FLUID because we kept seeing the same pattern: smart founders, brilliant products, and zero online presence. Not because of bad marketing — because of a missing foundation.

So we built a system that fixes that — brand, web, and discoverability, all working together. We called it Fluidity. And we built an agency around it.
