HOW WE WORK

No mystery. No chaos.
Just a clear path forward.

Every project follows the same four steps — and every Fluidity retainer follows the same four-week cycle. You'll always know exactly where we are and what's next.

ONE-TIME PROJECTS

From Day 0 to delivery.

Every project — brand, web, or both — follows this four-step process.

01

Discovery & Strategy

We kick off with a deep-dive discovery call to understand your goals, your audience, and your competitive landscape. From this we build your strategic foundation — the "why" behind every design decision that follows.

This isn't a brief form. It's a real conversation that shapes everything.

Discovery Call Brand Audit Competitive Analysis Creative Brief Project Roadmap
02

Design & Concepting

Our designers go heads-down. For brand projects: three distinct identity concepts. For web projects: wireframes, then high-fidelity mockups. You see everything at every stage — no big reveals, no surprises.

We present work in context, with rationale. Not just pretty pictures.

Mood Boards 3 Concepts (Brand) Wireframes (Web) Hi-Fi Mockups Prototype
03

Refinement & Development

You choose a direction, we refine it. Two rounds of revisions are included — structured and purposeful, not open-ended. For web projects, we move from design to Webflow build, with a staging environment you can review at any time.

Revision Rounds (×2) Webflow Build CMS Setup QA Testing Staging Review
04

Launch & Handoff

We launch your project, hand over all files, and make sure you know how to manage everything. You get a full handoff package — source files, guidelines, credentials, and a walkthrough video for your team.

This is where most agencies stop. For us, it's just the beginning — Fluidity starts here.

Live Launch Source Files Brand Guidelines Handoff Video 30-Day Support
FLUIDITY RETAINERS

The 4-week cycle that compounds.

Retainer clients run on a four-week cycle — audit, strategy, design, review — forever improving your brand's performance.

Week 1 — AUDIT

What's working?

We analyze your brand performance — traffic, conversions, AI mentions, search visibility, and competitor movement.

Week 2 — STRATEGY

What do we change?

Based on the audit, we prioritize the highest-impact improvements for this cycle and present a plan for approval.

Week 3 — DESIGN

We build it.

Our team executes the approved tasks — new pages, refreshed assets, updated messaging, AI-optimized content.

Week 4 — REVIEW

Did it work?

We measure the impact, document the learnings, and feed it into the next cycle's audit. Nothing is lost.
